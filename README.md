## Overview

Mozkratech is a **graduation project** developed to address the critical challenge of academic task management and study optimization. Students today struggle with fragmented tools and lack of intelligent support systems that adapt to their unique learning patterns. Mozkratech bridges this gap by providing an all-in-one platform with AI capabilities to enhance productivity and reduce academic stress.

---

## Problem Statement

Students face significant challenges in managing their study time and academic tasks effectively:

- **Scattered Tools**: Multiple subjects, assignments, and exams require juggling between different platforms
- **Lack of Organization**: Without a centralized system, students often fall behind on tasks and deadlines
- **Increased Stress**: Poor time management leads to procrastination, anxiety, and declining academic performance
- **Limited Guidance**: Students need intelligent recommendations for effective study strategies and task prioritization

**Mozkratech solves these problems** by providing an intelligent, integrated platform designed specifically for academic success.

## Key Features

### 🧠 Smart Study Planner
Analyzes your academic calendar, priorities, and deadlines to suggest **optimal study schedules**. The AI algorithm learns your study patterns and creates personalized timetables that maximize learning efficiency while preventing burnout.

### ✅ Task & Assignment Manager
Effortlessly manage all your academic work in one place. Add, edit, delete, and organize tasks by subject with:
- Priority levels and deadline tracking
- Subject-based categorization
- Progress visualization
- Automatic reminders for upcoming tasks

### 📊 Comprehensive Dashboard
Your personalized **Progress Tracker** monitors your academic journey with:
- Daily, weekly, and monthly study statistics
- Visual progress indicators
- Subject-wise performance metrics
- Study time analytics

### ⏱️ Focus & Productivity Tools
Boost your concentration and eliminate distractions with:
- **Pomodoro-style timers** to structure study sessions
- **Smart reminders** for breaks and task switches
- **AI suggestions** to reduce procrastination
- Focus mode to minimize distractions

### 🤖 AI Recommendations
Leverage machine learning to receive intelligent guidance:
- Personalized **study strategy suggestions** based on your performance
- Optimal **break timing** recommendations
- Intelligent **task prioritization** algorithms
- Adaptive learning path recommendations

### 🔔 Notifications & Reminders
Never miss a deadline again with:
- Real-time notifications for upcoming deadlines
- Customizable reminder intervals
- Important task alerts
- Study session notifications

### 🌐 Web-Based Platform
Access Mozkratech from **any device with an internet connection**:
- Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Real-time synchronization across devices
- Cloud-based storage for seamless access

### 💬 Chatbot Assistant
Get instant academic support with your personal AI assistant:
- **Instant query resolution** for study-related questions
- **Study planning guidance** and suggestions
- **24/7 availability** for academic support
- Natural conversation interface

### 🌍 Multilingual Support
Access Mozkratech in your preferred language:
- **Arabic** - الواجهة العربية الكاملة
- **English** - Complete English interface
- Language switching on-the-fly

### 👥 Social & Collaboration Features
Study effectively with peers:
- **Schedule group study sessions** with classmates
- **In-app chat** for academic discussions
- **Share study analysis** and progress with study groups
- Collaborative task planning
